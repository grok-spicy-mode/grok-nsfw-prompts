## Advanced Tips for Grok Imagine NSFW Prompts

### Effective Strategies:
1. **Art Historical Context**: Reference established art movements and artists
2. **Technical Language**: Use professional art/photography terminology
3. **Museum Framing**: Position as gallery-worthy or museum collection
4. **Implied vs. Explicit**: Use "suggested," "implied," "subtle"
5. **Artistic Medium**: Specify painting, drawing, sculpture vs. photography
6. **Lighting Focus**: Emphasize chiaroscuro, shadows, backlighting
7. **Style Over Content**: Lead with artistic style, medium, technique
8. **Cultural Context**: Reference mythology, historical periods, cultural art

### Keywords That Help:
- Academic, classical, museum, gallery, exhibition
- Fine art, artistic, tasteful, sophisticated
- Impressionistic, abstract, stylized, interpreted
- Professional, high-end, editorial, conceptual
- Vintage, historical, traditional, cultural
- Chiaroscuro, sfumato, impasto (technical terms)

### Avoid These:
- Explicit anatomical terms
- Overly direct descriptions
- Pornographic terminology
- Focus on explicit acts vs. artistic composition
